# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful and professional community.

## Expected behavior

- Use respectful language and constructive feedback.
- Be inclusive of different experience levels.
- Focus on collaboration, not personal criticism.

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, threats, or discrimination
- repeated disrespect or personal attacks
- leaking private information without permission

## Reporting

If you witness behavior that violates this policy, report it in a private issue or direct message with concrete details.
